Redesign this website using a bold, high-energy color system inspired by playful, mascot-driven web brands. The interface should feel alive, memorable, colorful, and emotionally immediate without becoming messy.

Use:
- large flat background color fields
- one dominant background color per section
- electric indigo for structure and primary CTAs
- hot pink for emphasis and playful accents
- soft white for text and pill buttons
- sky blue, lavender, and vivid blue-violet as rotating section backgrounds

Rules:
- keep the palette tight and intentional
- use high contrast
- avoid gray-heavy or corporate-looking sections
- make buttons chunky, rounded, and obvious
- allow one emphasized word or phrase in headlines using the accent color
- ensure illustrations, icons, or graphics echo the same palette as the interface
- make each section feel like a scene change with its own mood
- preserve readability and hierarchy
- do not overuse gradients, glassmorphism, or dark muddy tones

Target feeling:
fun, alive, premium-playful, internet-native, high-energy, visually sticky
